## Beyond MVG Application

The MS4 workshops were conducted entirely on the Beyond MVG identify
and prioritise application (no Miro board was used in MS4). The app is
the primary artefact for these sessions -- please go there directly to
explore the recommendations, ratings and discussion outcomes:

- App home: https://beyond-mvg.vercel.app/
- Data view: https://beyond-mvg.vercel.app/data
- Workshops page (cleaned summaries and ratings): https://beyond-mvg.vercel.app/workshops

Live RICE and MoSCoW assessments, comments and the "Discussed in
workshop" admin flags created during these sessions are surfaced on
the pages above.

A snapshot of the overview page 3D graph is included under
[`visuals/`](visuals/) as a static reference, but the interactive app
is the canonical view.
